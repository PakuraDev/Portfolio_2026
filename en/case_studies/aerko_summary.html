Let's summarize what Aerko_ is, a bit about its design and its code. I won't use technical language here — there's enough of that in the case studies you can read (unless you're on mobile, in which case you can only read this).

What is Aerko_?

Aerko is a fitness PWA (installable web application) that combines training, nutrition, and progress in one place. It's a tool designed for advanced users (though anyone who goes to the gym could use it without issues) optimized to manage your life in the rawest and fastest way possible.

Design

Visually, Aerko is a brutalist app, which often feels intimidating or "ugly," but it's actually a very simple app that prioritizes utilitarianism and speed over beauty. The entire interface is calculated for the user, from navigation and elements designed for safe thumb reach, down to the smallest details like its own numeric keypad with "Anti-shake" logic so you don't double-press a key when your hands are trembling after a heavy set.

Code

Aerko is built with modern technologies and optimized for extreme performance. Everything happens on your device, which can become a safe if you decide to encrypt your data with your PIN thanks to the cryptography system. Additionally, it can run AI models to analyze your video technique locally and efficiently (it runs on low-end devices from 2018-2019 — and that limit is due to technologies and dependencies, not components), so even a "potato" phone won't catch fire.
